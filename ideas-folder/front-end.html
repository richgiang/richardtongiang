TWOCITIES
Two Cities is a project built to comment on the disparity in the geography of Columbus, Ohio. Between education attainment, green spaces, and race there exists an underlying, systematic segregation of Columbus' populations along an all-too-familiar line.
BLACK VOICES
Black Voices is a landing page created to house the articles written in collaboration between the Lantern Newspaper and the BlackxBold Magazine publications. The special project was created to highlight the successes of Black individuals of the Ohio State community in celebration of Black History Month and beyond.
