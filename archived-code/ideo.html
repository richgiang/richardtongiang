Here's a bit about why I'm psyched about IDEO.org, why I love storytelling, and some details about me and my work.
Lately I've realized that I've always been a much better writer than a speaker (don't let the TEDx talk fool you, I've never practiced anything more in my entire life). My brain sometimes just moves too fast for coherent sentences to come out (think about an 8-lane superhighway being redirected to one-lane because of an accident, but not as morbid). Across this website are a bunch of my reflections, ideas, work, and some random thoughts that get at how I think I could bring my unique perspective as a child of Vietnamese refugees to contribute to the collective of story-centric work and culture at IDEO.org.

Please, feel free to explore and I hope you enjoy! :)
