UX Design contracting for a local metabolic disease management startup
For summer of 2022 I've been contracted by a local Columbus startup called Empower Health that recently acquired funding through an accelerator to be their temporary UX Product Lead. Empower Health is a mobile application company focused on providing a digital community for those living with metabolic diseases. In this role I'm leading their efforts to better understand their user's stories and needs with the goal of producing a click-through prototype for MVP development by the end of my contract.

Timeline

May 2022 - Present

Collaborators

Anjali Prabhakaran (CEO), Chase Fensore (CTO), Morgan Nemcek (CMO), and Angelina Atieh (CSO)

Skills

User Research, Mixed Methods Research, Journey Mapping, User Persona Development, Wireframing, Figma, Design Workshopping, React.JS, HTML/CSS/JS, Brand Identity Development

Problem

Those living day-to-day with metabolic diseases only come into contact with their physicians only a few times per year. Because of this, there is a lack of a connection that encourages patients to work towards their own betterment.

Solution

An automated goal maintenance software that groups users together with similar goals for the purpose of increased accountability towards health benchmarks and a sense of community and connection in the gaps between physician visits.

My Role

Lead all design initiatives in a long-term contracting role from user research and ideation to prototyping and building mobile app solutions
